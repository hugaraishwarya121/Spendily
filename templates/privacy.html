Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Your email address and password (stored securely as a hash) when you register.
  • Expense records you create: amount, category, date, and any description you provide.
  • Basic usage data such as the date your account was created.

We do not collect payment information, device identifiers, or browsing behaviour beyond what is needed to operate the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly for you:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To allow you to filter, edit, and delete your records.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed before being saved. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or data brokers.

The app uses Google Fonts (DM Serif Display, DM Sans) to render text. When your browser loads these fonts, Google's servers receive your IP address as a standard part of the web request. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond as promptly as we can.
